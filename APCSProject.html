How to Play:

  1. Click Start Game to start the game
  2. Look at the silhouette of the country and click Guess Country to guess
  3. You have 5 tries to guess the mystery country
  4. If you can't guess the country in 5 tries you lose
  5. Make sure to capitalize the first letter in the country's name
